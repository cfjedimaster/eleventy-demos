Title: Buttermilk Pancakes
Description: 
Source: Food Network Magazine
Original URL: 
Yield: 
Cookbook: Breakfast
Section: Main
Image: 
Ingredients: 
	6 cups flour
	2/3 cup sugar
	2 teaspoons salt
	2 teaspoons baking powder
	1 teaspoon baking soda
	2 eggs
	5 cups buttermilk
	2 sticks butter
	2 teaspoons vanilla
Instructions: 
	Whisk flour, sugar, salt, baking powder, baking soda in a large bowl.
	Whisk eggs, buttermilk, cooled melted butter and vanilla in another bowl.
	Pour wet ingredients into dry.
	Butter my nonstck frying pan.
	Pour 1/2 cup full of batter into pan, cooking about three minutes or until bubbling. 
	Flip and cook another minute or so.
