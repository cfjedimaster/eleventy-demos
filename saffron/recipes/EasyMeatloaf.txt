Title: Easy Meatloaf
Description: This is a very easy and no fail recipe for meatloaf. It won't take long to make at all, and it's quite good!
Source: Allrecipes.com
Original URL: https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/16354/easy-meatloaf/
Yield: 16 servings
Cookbook: Meats
Section: Ground Beef
Image: 
Ingredients: 
	3 pounds ground beef
	2 egg
	2 onion, chopped
	2 cup milk
	2 cup dried bread crumbs
	salt and pepper to taste
	4 tablespoons brown sugar
	4 tablespoons mustard
	2/3 cup ketchup
Instructions: 
	Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
	Sautee onions in oil and butter before adding into mixture. 
	Combine the beef, egg, onion, milk and bread crumbs. Season with salt and pepper to taste and place in a lightly greased 9x5-inch loaf pan, or form into a loaf and place in a lightly greased 9x13-inch baking dish.
	In a separate small bowl, combine the brown sugar, mustard and ketchup. Mix well and pour over the meatloaf.
	Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 1 hour.
