Ingredients: 
	2 cups of flour
	pinch of salt
	1/4th cup of sugar
	4 teaspoons of baking powder
	1 and 1/3rd cup of milk
	1/2 cup of peanut butter
Instructions: 
	Add dry ingredients and stir.
	Add milk and stir.
	Add peanut butter and stir.
	Pour into baking pan. (Guy used a knife to cut down the middle)
	Bake at 325 for 70 minutes.
