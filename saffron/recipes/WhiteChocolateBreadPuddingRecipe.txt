Ingredients: 
	1 loaf of French bread (stale)
	1 1/2 cup of sugar
	3 cups of whole milk
	4 eggs
	1 tbsp vanilla
	1 stick of unsalted butter (melted)
	1 cup of white chocolate chips
	1 tsp cinnamon
	RUM SAUCE INGREDIENTS:
	2 cups of evaporated milk
	1 cup of sugar
	3 egg yolks
	1 shot of rum
Instructions: 
	In a large mixing bowl, break bread into small pieces. Add all ingredients, mixing well. Place ingredients into a medium deep greased baking dish and bake at 325Â° for 45 minutes.
	Rum Sauce
	In a medium sauce pan, add all ingredients and mix well. Cook on medium-high until mixture begins to boil. Reduce heat to a low simmer. Allow to slow cook for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat and pour over warm bread pudding.
	Best enjoyed with a cup of hot coffee!
